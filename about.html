About Us

ItalianWineConcepts was born as the brainchild of famed sommelier Emmalino DeVino, who in 2020, found their calling to wine so strong it could no longer be ignored. Emmalino quit their job as a sommelier to the stars in Tuscany to pour heart and soul in IWC. The blog stands as the authority of Tuscan wine culture, producing reviews, recommendations, and pairings.

The goal of IWC is to show the world the beauty of Tuscany; the finest grapes produce the finest wines. Emmalino has devoted their entire life to learning Tuscan wine from seed to stem. IWC is the result of this dedication.

What we do

IWC partners with vineyards and internationally recognized wine locales to give its readers every detail possible. Hopefully, you will taste the expertise in each recommendation you take from IWC. Our wines will have you saying, “Mama mia!”
