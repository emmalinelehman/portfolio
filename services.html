Services

  • Tastings

    ItalianWineConcepts is proud to host the finest tasting sessions. We invite individuals to bring a group of friends and loved ones to celebrate the beauty of Italian wines. A tasting is typically hosted as a three hour event, with sips offered of a variety of curated wines. These sessions make fantastic day trips, honeymoon highlights, or birthday celebrations. Your group will be educated about each wine’s notes and history, given appropriate servings of numerous varieties, and encouraged to take home the favorites. Please book at least two weeks in advance to ensure your spot.

    Pricing: $300 - $2,000

  • Education

    Wine is a classical skill. Take one of our classes, offered on site of Emmalino DeVino’s storied vineyard. Classes review the history of Tuscan wines, beginning pairing techniques, correct aeration technique, among many other skills. These classes make excellent date nights or girl’s night activities. It is recommended that you commit to attending at least four classes to truly develop the mind of a sommelier. Please reach out to the sommelier’s of IWC for further questions or scheduling help.

    Pricing: prices vary per group

  • Curated Wine lists

    Have the perfect menu planned for that big event, but no clue how to pair the right wine? Let us do the work for you! Send us your menu and ingredient descriptions and we will be happy to create bespoke curated wine lists for your event. Fish, cheese, steak- we can pair anything! Our expert tastebuds will help you elevate your meal from good to grape! All wines listed will be available to purchase through ItalianWineConcepts. Delivery fees waived if the entire list is purchased.

    Pricing: Varies on selection
